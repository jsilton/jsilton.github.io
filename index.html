About Me

Product, SEO, Innovation

Hi, I'm Jordan. I have a passion for developing exceptional teams, building innovative products, and solving complex business problems. I play tennis and soccer, love sailing, and enjoy cooking.

I am VP, SEO for Angi (NASDAQ: ANGI). In this role, I lead the organic growth vision, strategy, and execution for both homeowners and pros.

Prior to this role, I served as Senior Director of Product, SEO at CoStar Group where I led the product organization for Apartments.com and championed organic growth as well as renter and customer experience for the nation’s most comprehensive online rental marketplace and extensive network of 12 leading sites.

Before working at Apartments.com, I crafted and executed SEO, analytics, paid search, retargeting, and additional digital marketing strategies for clients such as Alston & Bird, AT&T U-verse, Atlanta Ballet, Ashton Woods Homes, Cartoon Network, GameStop, Kimberly-Clark, Lowe’s, NASCAR and Zaxby’s while at Search Discovery and Relevance Advisors.

For school, I attended Emory University and hold a bachelor’s in business administration with concentrations in Marketing and Organization and Management from the Goizueta Business School and a minor in Theater Studies from Emory College.

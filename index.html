About Me

Marketing, Product, Growth

Jordan Silton is a growth-oriented executive who builds and scales the teams, products, and marketing programs that drive business performance. Currently, Jordan serves as Chief Marketing Officer at Power Support Partners, a company building America's most empowering home generator business by uniting the Southeast's top local service teams with the capital, technology, and people-first playbooks to deliver unmatched service and reliability.

Previously, Jordan served as the Vice President, Organic Growth & Integrations at Rent. (a Redfin subsidiary), where he drove marketplace expansion and reversed organic traffic declines across Rent.com and ApartmentGuide.com. At Angi, as Vice President of Product, he contributed to the company's return to profitability by establishing the product growth organization and enhancing demand generation and conversion. During his tenure at CoStar Group, he served as the Senior Director of Product, playing a crucial role in achieving record-breaking milestones and propelling Apartments.com to the forefront of the apartment rental industry. Earlier in his career, Jordan held leadership positions at Relevance Advisors and Search Discovery, successfully executing digital marketing campaigns for clients such as AT&T, Chick-fil-A, GameStop, and Zaxby's.

Jordan holds a BBA in Marketing & Managing Organizations from Emory University's Goizueta Business School. He speaks at conferences in the product, marketing, and multifamily industries and has mentored emerging leaders and judged awards across the technology and marketing industries.

Beyond his professional endeavors, Jordan has a deep passion for theater and the arts and is active in Atlanta's theater community. In his spare time, he plays sports like soccer, tennis, and sailing. He also enjoys cooking and experimenting with new recipes. Originally from Boston, MA, Jordan now calls Atlanta, GA his home, where he lives with his wife and three children.

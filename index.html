About Me

Product, SEO, Innovation

Jordan Silton is a highly skilled and accomplished product executive with a proven track record of driving business growth through innovative solutions and exceptional team building. As VP, Product at Angi (NASDAQ: ANGI), Jordan leads the product growth efforts for homeowners, utilizing his expertise in search engine optimization, editorial content, analytics, and customer experience.

Prior to his role at Angi, Jordan served as Senior Director of Product, SEO at CoStar Group (NASDAQ: CSGP), where he led the Apartments.com product organization and advanced the growth strategy of the nation's most comprehensive online rental marketplace. Under Jordan's leadership, Apartments.com earned over 1.2 billion renter visits and generated 120 million leads for customers in 2021, launched the groundbreaking Listing of the Future, and rolled out integrations with over 8,800 apartment communities, enabling renters to schedule over 730 million tours.

Before his time at CoStar Group, Jordan held leadership roles at Relevance Advisors and Search Discovery, where he developed and executed successful digital marketing campaigns for clients such as Alston & Bird, AT&T U-verse, and Zaxby's.

Jordan holds a bachelor's degree in business administration with a focus on marketing and organization and management from Emory University's Goizueta Business School, as well as a minor in Theater Studies from Emory College. He is a member of the Effie Worldwide judging panel and served on the Emory Alumni Board. In his free time, Jordan loves going to the theater, participating in sports such as tennis, soccer, and sailing, and he also enjoys cooking and trying out new recipes.

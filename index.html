Hi there, I'm Jordan Silton!

I have a passion for problem solving and digital marketing, play ultimate frisbee and enjoy cooking.

I am Senior Director, SEO & Analytics at CoStar Group (Nasdaq: CSGP), the leading provider of commercial real estate information, analytics and online marketplaces. I lead the search engine optimization (SEO), content, and data science practices for the Apartments.com Network of rental marketplaces including Apartments.com™, ForRent.com®, Apartment Finder™, ApartmentHomeLiving.com™, Apartamentos.com™, WestsideRentals.com™, ForRentUniversity.com®, AFTER55.com™ and CorporateHousing.com™.

Before working at Apartments.com, I crafted and executed SEO and digital media strategies for clients such as Alston & Bird, AT&T U-verse, Atlanta Ballet, Ashton Woods Homes, Cartoon Network, GameStop, Kimberly-Clark, Lowe’s, NASCAR and Zaxby’s while at Search Discovery and Relevance Advisors.

For school, I attended Emory University and hold a Bachelors in Business Administration with concentrations in Marketing and Organization and Management from the Goizueta Business School and a minor in Theater Studies from Emory College.
